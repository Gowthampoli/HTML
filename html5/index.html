About bengaluru

Bengaluru (formerly Bangalore) is the capital of Karnataka, India, and a major hub for the nation's IT industry, earning it the nickname "Silicon Valley of India". It is a megacity with over 10 million people, located on the Deccan Plateau at an elevation of over 900 meters, making it the highest of India's major cities. Beyond its technological significance, Bengaluru is also known for its pleasant climate.
